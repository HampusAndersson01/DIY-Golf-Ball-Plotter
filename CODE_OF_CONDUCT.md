## Code of Conduct

This project aims to be a practical, respectful place to collaborate.

### Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, reviews, and issue reports.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent, while still being clear about technical concerns.
- Share relevant context when reporting bugs, failures, or safety issues.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Abusive, insulting, or deliberately inflammatory language.
- Repeated disruption, bad-faith argument, or intentional misrepresentation.
- Publishing private, sensitive, or security-relevant information without permission.

### Reporting

If you experience or witness a problem, report it to the project maintainer through the repository contact channel or another clearly documented maintainer contact method.

### Enforcement

Project maintainers may review reports, request clarification, moderate discussions, remove inappropriate content, or restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.

Enforcement decisions should be made in good faith and with reasonable attention to context, safety, and impact.
